Building infrastructure in 'real' clouds

We are working on a highly-scalable and integrated infrastructure ecosystem for urban air mobility

Bringing a new perspective on rooftops

Not enough rooftops are being efficiently utilised to create value for society. We are looking to use rooftop space to alleviate congestion, pollution and noise from cities' scarce and overused streets.

Infrastructure for Urban Air Mobility

The possibility of flying rooftop to rooftop has been dreamt of for decades. Today, the technological progress finally allows us to make this a reality.

However, infrastructure in our cities is not suitable for large-scale deployment of any aerial mobility systems, such as drones or eVTOLs.

Our goal is to create an integrated infrastructure ecosystem in the sky that enables deployment of urban air mobility and ensures safe and efficient operations for passengers and cities' residents.
